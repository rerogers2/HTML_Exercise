Robert Rogers

Robert Rogers is a mathematics teacher that is always looking for a challenge. He has spent most of his adult life working towards improving the intelligence of our young adults. His passion for mathematics began at an early age and was a driving force through his educational years. He graduated with a Mathematics degree from Texas A&M University. Robert stumbled into teaching and used that time to pursue knowledge in other fields and grow an interest in programming. He is currently working on computer engineering. His goal is to challenge himself further by applying his new skills while improving the quality of life for others.
